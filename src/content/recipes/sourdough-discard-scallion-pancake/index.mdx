## Ingredients
* 3/4 cup (170g) sourdough starter, unfed/discard
* 2 tablespoons (28g) water
* 1/4 teaspoon table salt
* 1 teaspoon baking powder
* 2 scallions, white parts chopped finely and green parts sliced about 1/4" wide
* large handful of fresh herbs, coarsely chopped (cilantro, parsley, dill, tarragon, chives, etc. ...)
* vegetable oil, for frying

## Method
* Mix everything
* Pan fry, on medium high heat.
* Serve with whatever
