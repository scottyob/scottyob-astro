## Ingredients
* Tortilla chips
* Chilaquiles sauce, or, green salsa
* Cotija Cheese
* Red onion
* Avocado
* Sour Cream
* Garlic clove, minced
* Egg if you like.

## Method
* Satay onion, for 5 minutes or so
* Add garlic, 30 seconds or so until fragrant. 
* Add sauce to heat, don't go overboard, a little goes a long way (goes soggy otherwise)
* Mix in chips
* Top with everything else.
